Vancouver Washington Makerspace
VanWaMakers
Our mission is to cultivate an inclusive, community operated Makerspace where creativity, collaboration, and shared knowledge empower individuals to learn, build, and innovate together. We are committed to equitable access, hands-on education, and mutual support — providing the tools, resources, and community connections that transform ideas into reality.
The Vancouver Washington Makerspace is just getting started! Although we don't have a space yet, this is your opportunity to help shape our community from the ground up. Whether you'd like to be part of creating the makerspace or simply follow our progress, we invite you to join our email list or Discord server. You'll receive updates on our planning meetings, milestones, and opportunities to get involved.
